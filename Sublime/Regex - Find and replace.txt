Find:  ([a-zA-Z]+)([,])([0-9]+)([,])([0-9a-zA-Z ]+)
Note:
	() = capture,
	[] = these character ranges, symbols, etc...
	+ = more than one character, i.e. unitl it does not match the pattern

Replace: \5\4\3\2\1
	\ = capture group id/number
